What is INTERIOR DESIGN ?

                   Mainly Interior Design refers to Designing space efficiently, inside four walls. It includes, electrical, plumbing, flooring, ceiling, walls, windows, electronics, furniture, kitchen systems, etc. Some of them overlap with architectural services like electrical and plumbing, window design (Since it affects the elevation of the building). And every time it feels attractive to all. Efficient  use of space and expenses according to budgets.

Design process

Step 2: Identify the best natural lighting

Step 4: Decide on colours

Nautical Interior Designing Style

Nautical interior design is the perfect way to make us feel like the ocean is always near. The characteristic features of nautical interior are the presence of textile, inexpensive wooden furniture and an abundance of white color.

Contemporary Interior Design style

Contemporary style encompasses a range of styles developed in the latter half of the 20th century. Pieces feature softened and rounded lines as opposed to the stark lines seen in modern design. Interiors contain neutral elements and bold color, and they focus on the basics of line, shape and form.
